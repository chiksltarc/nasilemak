History of Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak originated in Malaysia and is considered the national dish of the country. It was traditionally served as a humble breakfast dish, wrapped in banana leaves. Over the years, it has evolved into a popular dish enjoyed at any time of the day and is often found in street food stalls, restaurants, and even upscale dining establishments.

Ingredients

  • Fragrant rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Pandan leaf
  • Anchovies
  • Peanuts
  • Cucumber
  • Egg
  • Chili sambal
